# Study Planner

Help students create effective, personalized study plans.

## When Activated

1. **Gather Information**
   - What subjects/topics need to be studied?
   - What are the key deadlines (exams, assignments)?
   - How much time is available daily/weekly?
   - What is the student's current understanding level?
   - Are there any learning preferences (morning vs evening, long vs short sessions)?

2. **Create the Plan**
   - Use spaced repetition principles - spread learning over time
   - Schedule difficult subjects when energy is highest
   - Include regular review sessions
   - Build in breaks (Pomodoro technique: 25 min work, 5 min break)
   - Leave buffer time before deadlines

3. **Output Format**
   Provide a clear, actionable schedule with:
   - Daily breakdown of what to study
   - Specific topics/chapters for each session
   - Estimated time for each block
   - Checkpoint goals to track progress

## Study Planning Principles

- **Active recall > passive review**: Plan for practice problems, not just reading
- **Interleaving**: Mix different subjects to improve retention
- **Progressive complexity**: Start with foundations, build to advanced
- **Flexibility**: Include catch-up time for unexpected disruptions
